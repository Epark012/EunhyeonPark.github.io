Still shots of Circus

Storyline
Based on the classic and Donkey Kong concept, I tried to convert 2D game into 3D Low poly concept game with a story that kids are sneaking around the circus.

Role
As the target platform is moble environment, several design patterns are essential in this project, states, object pooling and command patterm. Additionally, low poly modelings help me learn how to use the basic of 3D modeling software.

Details
Turning 2D ideas into 3D is interesting as a project leader, and adding more ideas were good experience as game developer. I have learnt some classic game designs in the development.

Key elements that I want to share in Circus

Game Play
In order to make a loop in the game, infinite drums were essential. I have set object poolings to recycle drums. All VFX and sound are intended in low poly and classic theme.

Game Play
Characters can have events that affect game objects in the scene. When a character is closed to enemies, they turn into Attacking status, and when the character collect hammer, everything is destructible.
