Still shots of Neverland

Motivation
Based on my personal memory with grandmother, I wanted to make a virual experience re-creating my grandmothers memory based on her lastings. As interacting with hers, I want to share the precious memories and make her dream immersive.

Storyline
According to her memories, I have divided five steps to get fully virtual stories in her world. As I found her paintings in her room, the room will collapse and we will be exposed to her imaginary world.

Role
As a contributor and developer, I have focused on the her background. Her dementia made her more childish and pure, so that I used Quill VR to make animation and Unity timeline for smooth animations.

Key elements that I want to share in Neverland

Storyline
This story is based on her paintings when she was sufferred by dementia. While her desease got serious, I thought her drawings reflect her pure and innocent memories from her life. By programming virtual interaction, her narratives will be triggererd.

VR Design
Following the theme of Neverland, I have decided to use Quill VR for imaginary and vivid effect like painting-drawn style model. Models are created in Quill, animated in Houdini and compiled in Unity. This pipeline has given satisfactory results.
