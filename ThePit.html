Still shots of The Pit

Storyline
the era, where robots rule human, a robot is found in the grandfather's old and dark room. In the robot system, there is a secret of breakthrogh that change everything in the world.

Role
I led this project from the storyline to the graphic development including scriting and creating interaction design. By using Unity URP pipeline, optimising and increasing visual quality is the priority of my tasks.

Details
Inspired by VR simulation and VR experiences, I want to make an immersive storytelling project with realistic and futuristic visual with Blender, Substance Painter and Houdini for Graphic pipeline in Unity URP.

How does Graphic Software are used in The Pit

Project Programming

Based on Unity VR Toolkit, various interactions are implemented. In order to create realistic and fully immersive interaction, users are guided to follow act to trigger events based on the VR headset and controllers.

Graphic Pipeline
3D modelings and basic animations by Blender and Maya, Textures by Substance Painter and Visual Effect by Unity Shadergraph and Houdini. The pipeline is established for the performance of Oculus Quest2, low cost, high quality is the priority.
