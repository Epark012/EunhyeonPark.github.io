Current Project - MAL UCL

This project takes place in partnership with the British Museum’s Endangered Material Knowledge Programme (EMKP) and UCL Grand Challenges.

By creating an interactive museum experience in Unity 3D, curated in direct dialogue with indigenous elders, we explore both the role of VR in research as well as the ways shamanic concepts push the boundaries of the digital.
